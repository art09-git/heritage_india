Cultural Context

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is one of the most significant festivals in Hinduism. It symbolizes the spiritual victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance. The festival is celebrated by lighting oil lamps, decorating homes, and sharing sweets with family and friends.
